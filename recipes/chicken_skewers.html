Chicken Skewers

Description

Grilled chicken thighs with scallions on skewers for the summer.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • ginger
  • scallion
  • garlic
  • honey

Steps

  1. marinate the chicken for 3-6 hours
  2. add to skewers with scallions in between
  3. grill for 6-8 min on each side
